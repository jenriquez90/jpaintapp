Welcome to the paint application

Register

  1. Click on the register link.
  2. Type your desired username and password.
  3. Login and start adding contacts.
  4. Chat with your friends!

Features

  • Send your friends a private message.
  • Start a group chat by adding contacts into your current conversation.
  • Draw! You can draw on a canvas provided per conversation.
  • Turn off your friends' canvas layer so you can concentrate on your drawing.
